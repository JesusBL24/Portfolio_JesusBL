About me

Born in 2003, I’ve been playing videogames since my 3 years old, internally developing a sense of how they are designed and the objective of their mechanics and decisions. As a kid, I also spent a lot of time playing with my LEGOs, imaging stories and worlds later on would take me to start writing and developing my interest in storytelling and art.

Recently, I have received my bachelor’s degree Game Design and Development at the Rey Juan Carlos University in Madrid. During these years I’ve been studying game design simple and advanced concepts, programming in C#, C++ and Unity Engine. Moreover, I participated with my friends in several game jams as designer and storyteller - even I was art director once.

This year my first multiplatform project ‘El Coco’ has been released , where I worked together with the Recotechnology S.L team as game designer and producer. Here I designed difficulty and progression systems, designed the narrative structure, implemented levels and enemies, leaded my team as producer and prepared the game pages for all platforms. This is just the start, my passion for games and learning desire leads me to look for new horizons and further develop my skills.
